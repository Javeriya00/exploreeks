NGINX Ingress Controller

The NGINX Ingress Controller is a powerful and flexible way to manage access to services in a Kubernetes cluster.

  • Traffic Routing: Directs traffic to appropriate services based on rules.
  • SSL/TLS Termination: Supports secure HTTPS connections with certificates.
  • Load Balancing: Distributes traffic efficiently across backend pods.
  • Rewrite & Redirect: Allows URL rewrites and path-based routing.
  • Authentication: Supports JWT, OAuth2, and other authentication methods.
  • Rate Limiting: Controls request rates to prevent overload.
